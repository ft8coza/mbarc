About

Since its inception in 2010 Mariette Barnard Architects has held true to its original mission to design dwellings that are financially, socially and environmentally sustainable to both our clients and the build environment.

This is reflected in our passionate dedication to our clients and we pride ourselves on personal involvement throughout the design process as well as during construction to ensure an end-product which meets such criteria.

We pride ourselves on delivering dwellings that are both location and site-sensitive. In addition to this, environmentally responsible design forms part of our core design principles.

When appointed by a client, we ensure that our role and responsibilities in terms of design criteria are clear, and the client wishes are considered along these parameters.

Our knowledge of design principles, paired with our years of experience in the build environment, ensures that our clients obtain the best possible professional advice and design.

Mariette Barnard Architects have designed and managed various residential projects. These have been completed in various upmarket residential estates in the Western Cape, Gauteng, Limpopo and Kwa- Zulu Natal.
